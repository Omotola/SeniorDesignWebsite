2016-2017 Senior Project - TILTED

Tilted is a 3D online multiplayer party game. The game features a world that's balanced at a single point, as if it's laying on top of a pin. There are four players total who play over a networking interface. Three players play as "Runner" characters, who have to work together to keep the map from flipping over, while still competing against each other to collect gems scattered around the world. Collected gems are deposited in a designated spot known as the Shrine. The first Runner to deposit up to 4 gems wins the game. The fourth Player is the 'Master' character who flies above the world and is able to destroy and place new trees around the maze to block a Runner's path, or disturb the balance of the world in an effort to tip it over and kill the Runners. The Master wins the game if he can successfully tip over the maze, killing all Runners.
